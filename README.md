# Mechatronics Workshop for NFU Taiwan (2020–2026)

A collection of teaching material and workshop resources developed for the National Formosa University (NFU), Taiwan.

## Workshop Material

* [Mechatronics Workshop Notes](https://pab47.github.io/mechatronics/mechatronics_workshop.pdf)

Topics include:

* Sensors and actuators
* Arduino programming
* Embedded systems
* Robotics and mechatronics fundamentals
* Hands-on laboratory activities

# UIC Mechatronics Course — ME 411

Course material for ME 411 at the University of Illinois Chicago (UIC).

## Course Website

* [ME 411 Course Page](https://pab47.github.io/mechatronics/spring23.html)

The course includes:

* Resistors, Capacitors, Inductors, Circuits
* Transistor, Op-Amps
* Logic Gates, Flip-Flop
* Sensors and Actuators
* Microcontrollers
* Mechatronics design projects
