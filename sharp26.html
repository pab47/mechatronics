SHARP 2026 Biomedical Engineering Internship

June 15, 2026 – July 24, 2026

Program Contacts

  • Principal Investigator (PI): Pranav Bhounsule (pranav@uic.edu)
  • Teaching Assistant: Nathan Morabito (nmorab2@uic.edu)

Overall Information

  • All projects are listed here: SHARP_2026.pdf
  • Students should obtain a laptop for all programming, documentation, analysis, and presentation work. Students should also maintain a dedicated lab notebook for notes, observations, experimental procedures, and results. Both the laptop and notebook should be brought to all sessions.
  • Students will present their progress every day using the following presentation template: Daily Presentation Template
  • The suggested timeline consists of approximately three weeks of laboratory training, literature review, and experimental study development followed by three weeks of experimentation, data collection, analysis, and report preparation. However, the final three weeks are especially important, so students are encouraged to complete the initial preparation phase as efficiently as possible.

Week 1

  • Introduction to Arduino in a Simulator (TinkerCAD)
  • Human Subject Research and Institutional Review Boards (IRBs)
  • Initial Literature Review

Week 2

  • Introduction to Arduino in Hardware
  • Extensive Literature Review and Research Study Development

Week 3

  • Sensors for Biomedical Research
  • Experimental Study Development Template
  • Complete the following form and email it to the PI: Study_2026.docx

Week 4

  • Instrumentation setup
  • Initial data collection
  • Preliminary data analysis

Week 5

  • Continued experimentation
  • Additional data collection
  • Data analysis and interpretation

Week 6

  • Final data collection and analysis
  • Report writing
  • Final presentation preparation
  • Preparation for the July 24, 2026 showcase
